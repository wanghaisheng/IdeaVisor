Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about IdeaVisor plans and features

Is IdeaVisor suitable for someone without design experience?

Yes, IdeaVisor is specifically designed for non-designers. Simply input a website link you like, and our system automatically extracts the design style and generates a prototype without requiring any design skills. Our visual editor also makes it easy to modify content and basic styles.

Can I use the generated design blueprints for commercial projects?

Yes, all design blueprints generated by IdeaVisor can be used for commercial projects. We extract design styles and structural inspiration rather than copying actual content, so you can confidently use the generated blueprints for your commercial projects and further customize them to suit your brand requirements.

Do I need to register to use IdeaVisor?

No. IdeaVisor offers a no-registration demo area where you can try our core features right away, input a URL, and view the generated design blueprint preview. However, to download the generated files or access more advanced features, you'll need to create an account. Registration is simple and quick, and we offer a perpetually free basic plan.

How is IdeaVisor different from other design tools or templates?

Unlike traditional design tools, IdeaVisor doesn't require you to design from scratch; unlike templates, we don't offer fixed options but analyze any website you like to extract its design essence. This allows you to build your website based on market-tested designs while maintaining uniqueness and customization, significantly shortening the time from concept to implementation.
